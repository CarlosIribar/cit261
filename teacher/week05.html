Web Mobile App Development

CIT 261

Instructor Preparation

Overview

The team activity for this week is build design and build a simple MVC application. The students will take the code from the Hikes demo if you shared it as a starting point. The discussion they have at the beginning is probably as important as the actual implementation. You may need to say a few words about MVC to help get them started on their discussions.

Encourage the students also to record their questions while reading and give them to you to be addressed.

Meeting Time

Keep your comments short this week in your meeting to allow for students to ask questions about their projects. It would probably be helpful to give more examples of using the browser to debug...breakpoints, stepping, etc.
